Property Research Locator

Search a U.S. city or street address to identify county-level research resources.

Find County Resources

This tool uses OpenStreetMap Nominatim geocoding and works for U.S. counties nationwide.

Enter a U.S. location to begin.
Nearby development activity (within ~5 miles) will appear after lookup.

How to use

  1. Search a U.S. city or specific street address.
  2. Open the county website to find local department pages.
  3. Use county clerk and assessor/appraiser links for records research.
No sample property records are included in this app.
